Getting Started

Welcome to NOVA! Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Sign up for a NOVA account
  • Download the NOVA app on your preferred device
  • Start browsing and watching your favorite movies and TV shows

Supported Devices

NOVA is available on a wide range of devices, including:

  • Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony, etc.)
  • Streaming devices (Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV, etc.)
  • Mobile devices (Android phones and tablets, iPhones and iPads)
  • Computers (Windows and Mac)

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues while using NOVA, try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Update your app to the latest version
  • Restart your device
  • Contact our customer support for further assistance
