Ways to Watch NOVA

Supported Devices

NOVA is available on a wide range of devices, including:

Smart TVs

Watch NOVA on your TV using compatible smart TVs from brands like Samsung, LG, Sony, and more.

Streaming Devices

Use streaming media players like Roku, Amazon Fire TV, Apple TV, and Google Chromecast to watch NOVA on your TV.

Mobile Devices

Download the NOVA app on your Android or iOS device to watch on the go.

Computers

Watch NOVA on your Windows or Mac computer using your web browser.

Casting and Screen Mirroring

You can also watch NOVA by casting or mirroring your device's screen to your TV using:

  • Google Chromecast
  • Apple AirPlay
  • Android screen mirroring

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues while watching NOVA, try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Update your app to the latest version
  • Restart your device
  • Contact our customer support for further assistance
