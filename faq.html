Frequently Asked Questions

What is NOVA?

NOVA is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of movies, TV shows, and more.

How much does NOVA cost?

NOVA offers several subscription plans starting at $8.99 per month.

Can I watch NOVA on my TV?

Yes, you can watch NOVA on your TV using compatible devices like smart TVs, streaming media players, or by connecting your computer to your TV.

How many devices can I use with my NOVA account?

The number of devices you can use depends on your subscription plan. Most plans allow you to stream on multiple devices simultaneously.

Can I cancel my NOVA subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your NOVA subscription at any time without any penalties or fees.

Does NOVA offer offline viewing?

Yes, NOVA allows you to download select movies and TV shows to watch offline on your mobile devices.

What is the difference between NOVA and other streaming services?

NOVA offers a unique combination of features, including a vast library of content, personalized recommendations, and a user-friendly interface.

Can I share my NOVA account with others?

Yes, you can share your NOVA account with others, but please note that each account can only be used by one person at a time.

How do I contact NOVA customer support?

You can contact NOVA customer support by visiting our Help Center or by sending an email to [support@nova.com](mailto:support@nova.com).

What are the system requirements for using NOVA?

NOVA can be used on devices with the following system requirements: Windows 7 or later, macOS 10.12 or later, Android 5.0 or later, and iOS 11 or later.
